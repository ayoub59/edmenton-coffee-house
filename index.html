FAQ
Edmonton Coffee House is dedicated to providing excellent service and a great customer experience. To help with this, we've compiled a list of frequently asked questions to assist you. Our hours of operation are from 6am to 10pm, 7 days a week. We offer a wide variety of coffee options, including drip coffee, espresso, lattes, cappuccinos, and more. We also serve breakfast, lunch, and pastries. For customers in need of internet access, we provide complimentary Wi-Fi. Additionally, we have a loyalty program where customers can earn points for every purchase and redeem them for discounts and free items. If you have any other questions, please feel free to ask one of our friendly staff members.
