About us
Nestled in the heart of Rabat, Edmonton Coffee House is a warm and inviting coffee shop that is passionate about serving the finest coffee blends and delicious food. Our skilled baristas handcraft each cup of coffee with locally sourced, freshly roasted beans, ensuring that every sip is rich and flavorful.

What is special about
Edmonton

Edmonton Coffee House is the perfect place to indulge your love for coffee in Rabat. Our cozy atmosphere, friendly staff, and commitment to quality make us the ultimate destination for coffee lovers. With a wide variety of coffee options, delicious breakfast, lunch and pastry offerings, there is something for everyone. We also provide complimentary Wi-Fi and a loyalty program to enhance the customer experience. 7 days a week, Edmonton Coffee House is the ideal spot to catch up with friends, get some work done, or simply enjoy a great cup of coffee.

Nestled in the heart of Rabat, lies a coffee shop that truly embodies the essence of this vibrant city. The brainchild of a Moroccan-Canadian entrepreneur, Edmonton Coffee House pays homage to the city that captured his heart.

Having lived in Edmonton, Canada for many years, the owner developed a deep love and appreciation for the people and culture of this bustling metropolis. With a passion for coffee, he set out to bring a taste of Edmonton to Rabat by opening a coffee shop that would capture the warmth and charm of this beloved city.

Edmonton Coffee House is not just a coffee shop, it is a celebration of the fusion between Moroccan and Canadian cultures. From the warm and welcoming atmosphere to the carefully crafted coffee blends and delectable menu offerings, every detail has been thoughtfully designed to reflect the unique spirit of both countries. Come and experience the perfect blend of Moroccan hospitality and Canadian charm at Edmonton Coffee House.
